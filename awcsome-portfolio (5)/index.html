about me.

Allan has always been passionate about language; it's natural flow, the ability to create stories, and how it provides the structures that frame an idea or direct an action. As the cornerstone of communication it leaves him in awe. After graduating from B. Property Economics at University of Technology Sydney he decided to pursue his passion. In the beginning it was his Wordpress blog that allowed him to cultivate his creativity, then, discovering programming, started his journey on Free Code Camp with the aim of becoming a front-end developer. In the face of an uncertain future he has developed patience, resolve, and a driven determination to become the best in his field.
